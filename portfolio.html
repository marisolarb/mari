Marisol Arboleda

NJIT, mia, B.Architecture

Marisol Arboleda is a first-year student at the New Jersey Institute of Technology’s Hillier College of Architecture and Design, where she is pursuing a bachelors in architecture. Marisol’s architectural work is focused on being as unique and creative as possible. Her goal is to push herself constantly to create the most captivating structures she is capable of making. She is also always ready to receive feedback and work with another colleague to improve. Before NJIT, she attended Paramus Catholic High School and graduated in 2023. She graduated as a member of the National Honors Society and has been awarded Principals List and multiple First Honors awards. While attending a private school in Paramus, she grew up in Ridgefield Park, New Jersey.
