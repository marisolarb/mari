The Foodie

Developed by Jacob Boden, Aisha Awad, Jennifer Avila, and Rachel Caladas

The Foodie is a wesbite designed mainly for students and faculty at NJIT.
Its purpose is to provide an array of good restaurants in the area so that students and faculty
looking for a quick bite can easily access nice places to go. The website is designed to link users
directly to the restaurants' menus, as well as their locations.
